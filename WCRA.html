The Black Espresso WCRA



The Black Espresso Worker and Consumer Rights Agreement is an agreement document that establishes the basic guaranteed rights, protections, and guidelines for both consumers and workers of Black Espresso.

Please do note, it is in early writing, and may not be perfect or finalized as of last updating.
